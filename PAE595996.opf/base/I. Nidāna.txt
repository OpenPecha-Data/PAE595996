ni brñes,sems can thams cad kyi ñon moṅs pa’i bag chags kyi mtshams sbyor ba legs par
’joms pa la ni mkhas,saṅs rgyas kyi mdzad pa lhun gyis grub pa ni rgyun ma chad ciṅ.
